A place for friends to share board games



How it works

At PlayLend we want to make it easy for people to lend and borrow board games from friends and other members in the community. The process is EASY! First you create a login and username in order to have access to the community, then once logged in, you will see a list of available games and who the respective owner. We encourage you to start adding games to your personal list and commece the sharing.
